*Last updated: March 19, 2026*

## What We Collect

- **Account information:** Your email address and password (encrypted) when you create an account
- **Chat history:** Your conversations with AI clones, stored securely on our servers
- **Device information:** Basic device type and OS version for troubleshooting

## What We Don't Collect

- We do **not** track your location
- We do **not** sell your data to third parties
- We do **not** use your data for advertising
- We do **not** share your conversations with anyone

## How We Use Your Data

- To provide the Clonebot chat service
- To improve response quality and app performance
- To respond to support requests

## Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure, private servers. Chat history is tied to your account and is only accessible when you're signed in.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at livenovastream@gmail.com.

## Third-Party Services

Clonebot uses AI language models to generate responses. Your messages are processed by these models to generate replies but are not stored by the AI provider.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.

## Contact

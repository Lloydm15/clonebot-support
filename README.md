## FAQ

**What is Clonebot?**
Clonebot creates AI versions of real people using their own words. Ask questions and get answers as if you were talking to them directly.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Your conversations are private and not shared with anyone.

**The app isn't connecting**
Make sure you have an internet connection. If the issue persists, email us.

**How do I report a bug?**
Email livenovastream@gmail.com with a description of the issue and your device model.
